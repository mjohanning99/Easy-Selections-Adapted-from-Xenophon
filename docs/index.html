Easy Selections Adapted from Xenophon

With a Vocabulary, Notes, and Map

Converted by Marvin Johanning; originally written by J. S. Phillpotts and C. S. Jerram

Phillpotts' and Jerram's Easy Selections Adapated from Xenophon is a great reader for those who have just started learning Ancient Greek and want to begin reading somewhat easier texts. This book was published in the mid-1800s and thus its copyright has expired. Therefore, freel free to use these as you wish, in accordance with the license stated below.
